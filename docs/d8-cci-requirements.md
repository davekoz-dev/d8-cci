D-8 CCI Portal - Complete Development Requirements Document
1. Project Overview
Project Name: D-8 Chamber of Commerce & Industry (D-8 CCI) Official Portal under Indonesia's Chairmanship 2026–2027

Purpose:
Build the official web portal for D-8 CCI that serves as the primary digital platform for private sector engagement, business cooperation, and chamber activities among D-8 member countries, positioned under Indonesia's D-8 Chairmanship 2026–2027.

Primary Goals:

Provide comprehensive information about D-8 CCI objectives, members, and activities

Facilitate business-to-business connections and trade opportunities among D-8 countries

Support Indonesia's 5 priorities during 2026–2027 chairmanship (economic integration, halal economy, blue economy & green transition, connectivity & digital transformation, organizational reform)

Serve as central hub for Business Forums, Trade Fairs, Halal Expo, and investment promotion events

2. D-8 CCI Institutional Information
2.1 Objectives and Means (11 Core Goals)
a) Economic Integration & Product Diversification
Support cooperation among business sectors in D-8 countries through diversifying commercial products to exchange among themselves to achieve economic integration and socio-economic development.

b) Industrial & Agricultural Development
Development of industrial and agricultural products in D-8 countries and promotion of quality and competitiveness concepts.

c) Multi-Sector Enhancement
Encourage enhancement of Trade, Service, Industry, SMEs, Entrepreneurship, Agriculture, Intra-Regional Tourism, and Handicrafts; promote collective self-reliance based on harnessing complementarities and establishing value/supply chains; propose mutually advantageous economic policies.

d) Investment & Joint Ventures
Make recommendations to safeguard economic and business interests of D-8 countries, promote investment opportunities and joint ventures among member countries.

e) Events & Forums Organization
Assist, contribute and encourage organization of and participation in Business Forums, Trade Fairs, Joint Display Centers, Exhibitions, Seminars, Conferences, Lectures, and Publicity Campaigns.

f) Knowledge Exchange
Promote exchange of commercial, technical, industrial management, and scientific information, education, and know-how among members.

g) Joint Research & Data
Promote joint research and development among D-8 member countries; collate, collect and disseminate statistical data.

h) Trade Facilitation
Act for trade facilitation to remove barriers hindering exchange of goods and services among member states; identify and propose measures to increase trade volume and economic cooperation.

i) Investment Climate Improvement
Contribute to improvement of investment climate in D-8 countries to enhance foreign investment.

j) Dispute Settlement
Provide means for settling commercial disputes that might arise among companies of D-8 CCI members.

k) International Relations
Strengthen relations with international trade organizations, bodies, unions, and similar chambers to promote private sector's role in socio-economic development.

2.3 History & Key Milestones
Timeline of D-8 CCI Development:

Date	Event	Significance
22-25 Feb 2001	Cairo, Egypt - 3rd Cairo Summit	1st Business Forum of D-8 CCI held. Article 29 of Cairo Declaration approved establishment of business forum recognizing vital role of private sector. Initial proposal step.
14 Feb 2004	Tehran, Iran - 4th Tehran Summit	2nd Business Forum. D-8 FCCI officially established. Iran appointed to manage Secretariat; Mr. A.R. Hanjani appointed as Secretary-General. Official founding date.
11 May 2006	Bali, Indonesia - 5th Bali Summit	3rd Business Forum. Emphasized visa facilitation, shipping/logistics, and Preferential Trade Agreement (PTA) to expand intra-D-8 trade volume.
3 July 2006	Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia - 6th Summit	4th Business Forum. Focused on 3 potential sectors: Biotechnology, Halal Industry, Renewable Energy. Proposed meetings of senior economic officials and Economics Ministers.
5 July 2010	Abuja, Nigeria - 7th Abuja Summit	5th Business Forum. Recommended governments provide financial resources and banking credits to business communities. Established USD 50,000 annual membership fee for D-8 Secretariat activities.
5 April 2021	Virtual Event	D-8 Business Forum organized virtually as sideline of 10th D-8 Summit.
25-27 July 2022	Dhaka, Bangladesh	Business Forum and Expo held.
26-27 Oct 2022	Abuja, Nigeria	First D-8 Forum on SMEs and Health Market held.
15 June 2022	Various locations	Year-long D-8 Silver Jubilee Celebration completed.

5. Brand & Visual Identity Requirements
5.1 Design System (from PDF Brand Guidelines)
Typography:

Primary font: Fira Sans

Bold: Headings

Medium: Sub-headings

Regular: Body text and captions

Define web type scale:

H1: 36-40px Bold

H2: 28-32px Bold

H3: 22-24px Medium

Body: 16-18px Regular

Caption: 13-14px Regular

Color Palette:
​

Primary Blue (Azure): #055090

Primary Turquoise: #00B3AA

Midnight Blue: #282561

Blue Aqua: #00B9F0

Blue Sky: #2BB5E9

Dark Grey: #414042

Soft White: #F7F8F9

Logo Usage:

Main configuration for headers/hero sections

Secondary configuration for co-branding with member chambers

Clear space: minimum 2x around logo

Prohibitions: no rotation, no outlines, no color changes, no effects, no placement on complex backgrounds

Supergraphic & Pattern:

Supergraphic (Jyoti concept): Use as section backgrounds

Pattern: 40% opacity, edge placement only

Never place body text directly on busy supergraphic

Use approved color combinations from guidelines

Photography:
​

Reflect Indonesian culture, cooperation, solidarity, prosperity

2-column layouts with people photos

Full-color, duotone, or framed with supergraphics allowed
